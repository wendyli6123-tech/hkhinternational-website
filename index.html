Shower Door Hinge - Glass to Wall

Premium square profile shower door hinge with factory-bonded recessed gasket technology. Self-centering mechanism ensures perfect door alignment. Ideal for residential bathrooms and commercial hotel projects.

Specification Value
Glass Thickness 3/8" - 1/2" (10mm - 12mm)
Opening Angle 90° - 105°
Door Weight - 2 Hinges 80 lbs (36 kg)
Door Weight - 3 Hinges 120 lbs (54 kg)
Maximum Door Width 32" (813mm)

  • • Factory-Bonded Recessed Gasket (Patent Pending)

  • • Solid Brass / Stainless Steel Construction
  • • Residential & Commercial Applications

Polished Chrome, Matte Black, Brushed Nickel, Gold, and custom PVD coatings.
