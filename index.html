About Us

Foshan HighLink International Trade Co., Ltd.

Located in Foshan, Guangdong — China's hardware manufacturing hub, HKH International Trading Co., Limited has been specializing in manufacturing bathroom glass hardware for over 20 years.

We adhere to the business philosophy of "Quality First, Customer Supreme". Our complete product range covers solid brass hinges & clamps, stainless steel fittings, sliding door systems, door handles, patch fittings, floor springs and more. Every product is strictly inspected before shipment to meet international quality standards.
